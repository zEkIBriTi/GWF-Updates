About Health Section

This section also comprises the Regional Referral Hospital and the objective of this section is to facilitate the provision of health preventive, curative, health development and social welfare in the Region. The Section is led by an Assistant Administrative Secretary who is also a Regional Medical Officer.


Functions of Health and Social Walfare Sector Section

This Section will perform the following functions:

  • Co-ordinate and advise on implementation of health policy in the Region
  • Coordinate health and social welfare matters in the region
  • Monitor proper management of health services provided by public and private sector hospitals and other health facilities in the Region
  • Build capacity to LGAs in health service delivery
  • Provide technical advice on preparation of HIV/AIDS combating plans
  • Provide technical advice on HIV/AIDS activities in the Region
  • Provide backstopping support during health epidemics in the region
  • Provide clinical services to inpatients and outpatients referred to by LGA hospitals
  • Provide curative specialist services
  • Provide expert/technical backstopping service to LGA hospitals and health centres during major communicable diseases epidemics
  • Provide referral laboratory services
  • Coordinate availability of adequate pharmaceuticals products for the hospital
