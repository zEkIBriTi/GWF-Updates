The Unit of Information and Communication Technology

The Unit Information Technology and Communications aims to provide professional services on the use of information technology and communications for the Regional Secretariat, in addition This unit is led by analyst systems Computers General who is responsible for the Regional Administrative Secretary. 

Responsibilities of Department / Section

The responsibilities of the Division of Information Technology and Communications :

  • Develop plan strategy on information technology and communications, guidelines and procedures to comply with the National Policy for Information Technology and Communications.
  • Advise the management of the Regional Secretariat on issues related to policies related to information technology and communication and implementation of the concept of the government network.
  • Advise the management of the Regional Secretariat on issues related to policies, guidelines and procedures relating to the figures.
  • Manage the development of information technology and communications and its implementation for the Regional Secretariat.
  • PMORALG Collaborate and coordinate and develop standards for information technology and communication for the "software and hardware" for the use of the MRS and Local Government Authorities.
    Enabling Local government authorities in developing and implementing programs / projects of information technology and communication.
  • Coordinate the implementation of the architecture and maintain / develop consumer-related Web sites and record keeping for the Regional Secretariat, ministry, departments, agencies and other stakeholders.
  • Providing and coordinating the support of information technology in the Secretariat of Regional and Local Government Authorities.
  • Evaluate, improve and manage the information technology and communications and management information systems / information rooted / programmed on the Regional Secretariat and Local Government Authorities.
  • Make training needs assessment on the management of information systems and information technology and communications and organizing capacity-building program .
  • Make assessment of the risk / losses relating to infrastructure, information technology and communication and system management and how to manage information.Secretariat Regional and Local Government Authorities. 
  • Develop and maintain regional site.
  • Using information technology and communication to handle data and information, to keep, to pass them various stages, analyzing, presenting, dissemination and preservation.
  • Prepare all matters relating to statistics on the Regional Secretariat and Local Government Authorities.
